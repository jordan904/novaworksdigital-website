Our Mission

Digital Excellence for Real-World Businesses

NovaWorks Digital was founded in 2024 by Jordan McIntosh with a clear goal: put smart, practical automation into the hands of the people who actually keep communities running.

We are builders, not bots. Every tool we ship is designed by a team that understands your world — missed calls on job sites, leads slipping through the cracks, follow-ups that never happen. We fix that.

No jargon. No bloated platforms. Just digital tools that save you time, win you more jobs, and let you focus on the work you are great at.
